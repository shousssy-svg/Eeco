Activity 4: My Town, My Rule

Instructions

In this activity, imagine that you are one of the leaders of your town. Your goal is to help protect the environment and make sure that people, animals, and nature can live together peacefully. Think about the activities that affect the ecosystem in your community. Then, create your own environmental rule that will help keep the balance in nature.
Your rule should include clear “Do’s” and “Don’ts” that people in your town must follow. Explain how your rule can help create a greener and healthier environment.
