Introductory Message

The EcoScientia: Web-based SEL-Integrated Module (Web-SELM) was carefully prepared to help you, our dear learners to understand and appreciate the impact of human activities in an ecosystems. It will guide you in exploring how people's actions can either protect or harm the environment and how you as a studentcan become an advocate of ecological balance and be a student with environmental sensibility.

The activities, discussions, and reflections in this module are designed to enhance not only your scientific understanding but also your social and emotional awareness toward the living world around you. You will be given opportunities to think critically, act responsibly, and reflect on your own role in caring for nature. Each part of this module has been in arranged in a way that allows you to learn independently and meaningfully. Read the instructions carefully, complete each task honestly and answer all activities on a separate sheet of paper.

If you find any part of this module, difficult, don't hesitate to ask your teacher or parent for guidance. Remember, learning in most meaningful when you take initiative and stay curious. Enjoy your journey as a young steward of the environment. Let this lesson remind you that small actions can make a big difference in keeping our planet healthy and full of life.

The scope of this module allows it to be used in different learning setups, whether online or home-based. The language used considers the diverse backgrounds of learners, and the activities are designed to help you think critically, reflect emotionally, and act responsibly as a yound steward of nature.
